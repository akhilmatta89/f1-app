About Formula 1 Management

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest class of international racing for single-seater formula racing cars. It is one of the most prestigious and popular forms of motorsport in the world, featuring cutting-edge technology, high-speed races, and some of the best drivers in history.

This app is designed to help you manage and compare Formula 1 drivers and teams. You can:

  • Add new drivers and teams to the database.
  • Update existing driver and team information.
  • Compare drivers based on their stats like age, race wins, pole positions, and more.

Whether you're a die-hard F1 fan or just getting started, this app is your go-to tool for all things Formula 1!
